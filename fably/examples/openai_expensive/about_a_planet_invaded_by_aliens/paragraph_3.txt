In the quaint village of Dazzlebrook lived a young Whimsian named Ello. He was small, even by Whimsian standards, and loved inventing gadgets. The news of the invasion troubled him deeply. That night, under the light of the twin suns, a brilliant idea sparked in Ello’s mind. He decided that he would save Whimsiloon, but he knew he couldn’t do it alone.
