Upon stepping through, Sir Whiskers found himself in the mystical realm of Whiskerland. There, cats from all walks of life floated gracefully through the air, and fish swam through streams of cream. Enchanted by the beauty and magic, Sir Whiskers quickly learned that Whiskerland was a utopia for cats. Yet, tales abounded of an ancient sorcerer cat named Macabre Mogg, who sought to imprison the feline inhabitants and seize Whiskerland's magic for himself.
